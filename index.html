Welcome to Zhenpeng Xu's Homepage

   

Zhenpeng Xu, Ph.D. (徐振澎)

Mechanical Engineer - at Atomic Semi and was a postdoc at Advanced Manufacturing and Metamaterials Laboratory (AMML), UC Berkeley. 7+ years of additive manufacturing expertise in mechatronics system development, structural and hybrid materials, and functional composites. Authored several high-impact publications related to 3D printing. Developed a multi-material 3D printing process for creating strong and lightweight materials supported by ORNL. Developed a scanning projection stereolithography system that enables large-scale high-resolution printing. Developed a 5-axis direct ink writing platform for fiber alignment, and a continuous 3D printing system for rapid manufacturing. 5+ years of experience in synthesizing stereolithography-compatible functional composites, such as carbon fiber, magnetic, and piezoelectric powders. Experience with mechanical testing, DMA, SEM. Experience with MATLAB, LabVIEW, C/C++, Python. Experienced in entrepreneurship.
